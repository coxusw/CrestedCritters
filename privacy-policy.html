Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Crested Critters handles information in connection with the Crested Critters Content Agent, an internal tool used to draft, review, schedule, and publish content to Crested Critters social media pages.

Who we are

Crested Critters is a small business focused on isopods, bioactive terrariums, botanicals, and related educational content.

What the Content Agent does

The Content Agent helps create draft Facebook Page content, including educational posts and meme-style posts. Drafts are stored in a private Google Sheet for review. Only approved posts may be published to a connected Facebook Page.

Information we collect or process

The Content Agent is intended for internal business use. It may process:

  • Facebook Page information needed to identify the Page being managed.
  • Facebook Page access tokens used to publish approved content to the Page.
  • Draft post text, captions, hashtags, image prompts, generated image links, and posting status.
  • Basic posting logs such as timestamps, post IDs, success messages, and error messages.

Information we do not collect

The Content Agent is not designed to collect personal information from Facebook users, customers, followers, commenters, or visitors. It does not sell personal information and does not use Facebook user data for advertising, profiling, or resale.

How we use information

Information is used only to:

  • Generate social media draft content.
  • Store draft content for review and approval.
  • Publish approved posts to the Crested Critters Facebook Page.
  • Maintain logs for troubleshooting and content management.

Where information is stored

Drafts and logs may be stored in Google Sheets and Google Drive. API keys and access tokens may be stored in Google Apps Script project properties. Access is limited to authorized Crested Critters administrators.

Sharing of information

We do not sell or rent information. Information may be processed by service providers used to operate the tool, such as Google services, OpenAI services, and Meta/Facebook services, solely for the purpose of drafting, storing, and publishing approved content.

Data deletion requests

If you believe the Content Agent has stored information related to you, you may request deletion by contacting us. Please include enough detail for us to locate the information.

Contact email: Contact_us@crestedcritters.com

We will review deletion requests and delete applicable information where required or appropriate.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, including limiting access to the Google Sheet, Google Drive folder, and script properties used by the Content Agent.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
